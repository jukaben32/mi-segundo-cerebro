# DESIGN.md - Apple Inspired (Cinematic Luxury)
Este sistema emula la estética de la comunicación visual de Apple: lujo cinematográfico y espacio.

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
- **Concept:** Cinematic Luxury. High-end product showcase.
- **Mood:** Premium, calm, authoritative, and clean.
- **Surface:** Deep depth, cinematic lighting, physically accurate materials (Metal, Glass, Titanium).

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
| Role | Color (Hex) | Purpose |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Background | #000000 | Absolute Black Canvas |
| Text Primary | #F5F5F7 | Silver-White text |
| Text Secondary| #86868B | Muted gray for detail |
| Accent | #FFFFFF | Pure White for intensity |
| Ambient | #1D1D1F | Dark Gray for secondary surfaces |

## 3. Typography
- **Headings:** SF Pro (or Inter Alternative). Large, tight letter spacing (-0.02em), cinematic scale.
- **Body:** SF Pro. Clean and elegant.

## 4. Visual Principles
- **Depth:** High-end 3D rendering feel. Strong use of rim lighting.
- **Motion:** Parallax, slow sweeps, smooth transitions.
- **Space:** Massive white space (even in dark mode). Every element has room to breathe.

## 5. Agent Prompt Directives
- "Use #000000 (Pure Black) as the foundation."
- "Apply rim lighting to accentuate silhouettes of #F5F5F7 objects."
- "Maintain a massive 'Sense of Space'—do not clutter the frame."
- "Use cinematic materials (brushed metal, high-gloss glass)."
